Recent News RRC Commissioners Assess More Than $1.2 Million in Penalties February 25, 2026 AUSTIN – The Railroad Commission of Texas assessed $1,277,320 in enforcement docket fines against operators and businesses at the Commissioners’ open meeting on Tuesday.
