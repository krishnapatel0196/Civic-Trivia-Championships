Visiting the Auditor’s Office at City Hall We're open Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. We accept in-person visits Tuesday to Thursday at City Hall in room 130. For more information about how to connect with us either in person or remotely, please see our contact information on our homepage or visit our contact information webpage.
