Source a high-quality photograph of the Texas State Capitol for the collection banner image.

**About the building:** The Texas State Capitol (1100 Congress Avenue, Austin) was completed in 1888. It is built of sunset red granite from Marble Falls, TX. Its defining feature is its height: 302.64 feet tall, intentionally built 14.64 feet taller than the US Capitol (288 ft). It is a National Historic Landmark. The dramatic height and distinctive red granite make it the most recognizable civic symbol of Texas and the right subject for this banner. The building was designed by architect Elijah E. Myers in Renaissance Revival style.

**Source options (in priority order):**

1. **Wikimedia Commons (Creative Commons -- preferred for quality):**
   - Search: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Texas_State_Capitol
   - Filter for freely licensed images (CC-BY or CC-BY-SA or public domain)
   - Many high-quality exterior photos showing the full building are available
   - A dramatic wide-angle shot showing the building's height is recommended (the height is the signature fact)
   - Note the license for the SUMMARY (attribution may be required for CC-BY/CC-BY-SA)

2. **Texas State Preservation Board (public domain for official images):**
   - URL: https://tspb.texas.gov
   - Official photographs of the Capitol

3. **Library of Congress HABS (public domain -- no attribution required):**
   - URL: https://www.loc.gov/pictures/search/?q=texas+state+capitol
   - These are public domain

**Image requirements:**
- **Subject:** Exterior of the Texas State Capitol showing the full dome and height -- the civic landmark, not an interior shot
- **Preferred composition:** Wide-angle showing the building's dramatic height, ideally from south approach (Congress Avenue axis). Dusk/golden hour lighting with warm tones would complement the #BF0D3E theme color and the red granite facade.
- **Aspect ratio:** Landscape, approximately 2:1 (check other banners: indiana-state.jpg, california-state.jpg, massachusetts-state.jpg are reference points)
- **Format:** JPG
- **File name:** `texas-state.jpg`
- **Destination:** `frontend/public/images/collections/texas-state.jpg`
- Crop or resize to match the landscape aspect ratio used by other banners if needed

**Steps:**

2. Crop/resize to landscape ~2:1 ratio matching other banner dimensions (check existing banners for exact pixel dimensions)
3. Save as `frontend/public/images/collections/texas-state.jpg`
