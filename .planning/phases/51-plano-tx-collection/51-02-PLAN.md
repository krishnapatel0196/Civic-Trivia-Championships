  <how-to-verify>
Review the generated Plano questions for quality and accuracy. Focus on the known pitfall areas from the research:

1. **Factual accuracy checks (blocking):**
   - No question attributes Collin County responsibilities to the City of Plano or vice versa (voter registration, elections, courts = County; planning, fire, parks = City)
   - No question implies the Mayor is the chief executive — the City Manager (appointed) is the chief executive; the Mayor presides over Council
   - Balloon Capital of Texas designation is attributed to Governor Bill Clements in 1980 (not another year or governor)
   - Incorporation year is 1873 (not another year)
   - Toyota HQ broke ground January 2015 (not 2014 or 2017 — 2017 is when it became operational)
   - JCPenney HQ moved from NYC to Plano in 1992 (not another year)

2. **Content balance checks:**
   - Corporate questions have civic hooks — not brand promotion or product-related
   - The collection represents more than just the corporate/affluent identity — look for history, community, Balloon Capital, founding story, Plano ISD
   - Questions feel like "dinner party" civic tidbits, not address/phone number admin trivia
   - Expiring/durable balance: roughly 50/50 (not skewing heavily toward current-officeholder questions)

3. **Difficulty calibration:**
   - The mix skews toward Easy questions (appropriate for Easy Steps design)
   - No question has an address or phone number as its answer
   - No questions where only a city employee would know the answer

4. **Expiring questions:**
   - Questions about Mayor Muns should have expiresAt set to "2029-05-01T00:00:00Z"
   - Questions about specific council members should have expiresAt set per their cycle (2027 or 2029)
   - City Manager name questions should have expiresAt null and be flagged for manual refresh
   - Structural questions ("What form of government does Plano use?") should have expiresAt null

5. **Anti-partisan check:**
   - No party labels or partisan framing in any question or answer
   - Leaders referenced by civic role, not political identity

**To review questions:** Use the admin UI or query the DB for questions with externalIdPrefix like 'pla%' and status = 'draft'.

**If you find issues:**
- Archive individual bad questions via the admin UI or the archive script
- If a specific factual topic has systemic errors (e.g., all county/city attribution is wrong), archive that cluster and describe what needs fixing so Claude can adjust the voice guidance and regenerate
